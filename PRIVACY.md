# Privacy Policy — LinkedIn Exporter

**Last updated:** June 15, 2026  
**Extension:** LinkedIn Exporter  
**Developer:** Cheeznology

## Overview

LinkedIn Exporter is a browser extension that reads profile information from LinkedIn pages you are already viewing and lets you copy or export that data on your device. This extension does not operate a backend server and does not sell user data.

## Data we access

When you open the extension on a LinkedIn profile page (`linkedin.com/in/...`), the extension may read visible page content, including:

- Full name  
- Headline  
- Location  
- Current company  
- Profile URL  
- Profile photo URL  

The extension only runs when you interact with it on a page you have open. It does not browse LinkedIn in the background.

## Data we store

The following data is stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`:

- Export history (up to 50 profiles)  
- Multi-profile queue (up to 100 entries)  
- Tags and notes you add to profiles  
- Export timestamps  

This data stays on your device. We do not receive it.

## Data we do not collect

LinkedIn Exporter does not:

- Collect passwords or LinkedIn login credentials  
- Read your messages or personal communications  
- Track browsing history outside the active profile tab you use with the extension  
- Log keystrokes, clicks, or mouse movement  
- Send data to external servers operated by the developer  
- Use remote code  
- Sell or share user data with third parties  

## Permissions

| Permission | Why it is used |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | Save export history, queue, tags, and notes locally |
| `activeTab` | Access the current tab when you open the popup |
| `https://www.linkedin.com/*` | Run a content script on LinkedIn profile pages to read visible profile fields |

## Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or transfer your data to third parties.

## Children's privacy

LinkedIn Exporter is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the extension changes. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any revision.

## Contact

For privacy questions about LinkedIn Exporter, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
